4th Year Project - Rule Based Interactive Chatbot

This project was based around Rule-Based Interactive Chatbots, the variety of platforms available to develop them on and their use cases. The final iteration of this project was developed using Dialogflow. Functionality of the final chatbot included, allowing users to book a table for a restaurant, taking their name, date of choice, time of choice, the number of guests they wished to book for and storing them in a backend database. The database that was used for this storage was Google’s Firebase ‘Realtime Database’.

Other functionality available to users included being able to view a food menu, which was pulled from the database, and also place a food order. This order was then also stored in the database. Users could also review their booking information or cancel their booking if desired which removes all of their data from the database.

As the project was broken into three iterations, iteration two used a competing platform to Dialogflow, 'Amazon Lex', offered by Amazon Web Services (AWS). The aim of iteration two was to compare the two development platforms after building similar systems on each.

Marketing Communications (MarComms)

MarComms was a large responsibility of mine during my internship. This role consisted of the sourcing and scheduling of all social media content across all of Graphic Mint's social channels including Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn and Google+. At the end of each month I then had to complete a variety of reports detailing the analytics from each social media platform and each of Graphic Mint's websites and then carry out a presentation in which these metrics would be discussed. Plans would then be set in place for any changes that needed to be made to the marketing strategy based on the month's performance. UXAcademy.ie - Web Development Support

UX Academy (uxacademy.ie) is one of four websites owned by Graphic Mint. During my internship the website received a full redesign which I was involved with. I received the new design for the website from the UI & Interaction Design team and was then tasked with developing this new design along with the UI Developer. This was a great experience as it allowed me to expand my practice of Web Development, learn and understand the process in which the design is broken down into sections and building blocks and then piecing it all together into the final build of the website. I learned a lot during this process under the supervision of Graphic Mint's UI Developer.

Dorsetcollege.com - Development Support & QA

This was a large client project that I was involved with during my internship. It consisted of building a new website for Dorset College located in Dublin City. The project itself spanned across roughly 4 months of my internship and it was a great to be part of such a substantial project. The website build utilised a Content Management System (CMS) called Drupal which was something I had never used before. Once I got a better understanding of Drupal, I was delegated pieces of functionality to work on. After working on this functionality, I would then present my work so that it could be tested, iterated on and then implemented into the final build of the website. Other roles and responsibilities delegated to me as part of this project included working alongside Graphic Mint's Content Strategist to organise and prepare all of the content for the website. I was also involved with Quality Assurance & Testing and Usability Testing towards the end.

Demo Reel - Storyboarding, Music Production & QA

This was a large project undertaken by the interns during our time in Graphic Mint. The aim of the Demo Reel was to give an overview, in the form of a video, of the core service offerings of Graphic Mint across their 4 initiatives. This included their UX/UI Design and Web, Branding & Customer Experience (CX) services, UXAcademy.ie, Usabilitytesting.ie and Participants.ie. The process of creating the final animation video included many stages. As with all projects, it began with an initial kick-off meeting where we were briefed on the expected outcomes of the project. We then went on to brainstorming, storyboarding and prototyping with many meetings and iterations in between. I was then responsible for the music production side of the project. I enjoyed this as I got to use my passion for music and also video editing to help bring Graphic Mint's demo reel to life.
